Biography

  • I am currently a second-year Ph.D. student at the College of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University. I received my B.S. degree from the College of Information Engineering at Zhejiang University of Technology in 2019.
  • My research interests include domain adaptation/generalization, causal inference, adversarial learning.
  • I am working with Associate Professor Kun Kuang now, and we are focusing on developing stable and interpretable machine learning approach via causal inference and improving causal inference method with deep learning. My tutor is Professor Lanfen Lin, and we worked on adversarial learning previously. Before that, I also worked with Professor Qi Xuan on network science.
