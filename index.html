Biography

I am currently a third-year Ph.D. student at the College of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University, supervised by Prof. Lanfen Lin, Kun Kuang, and Fei Wu.

I am focusing on promoting generalization of the machine learning systems. My research interests include domain adaptation/generalization and causal inference.
